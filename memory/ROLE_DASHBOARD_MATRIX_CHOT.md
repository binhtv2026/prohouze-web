# ROLE DASHBOARD MATRIX CHOT

## Muc tieu
- Chot lai `nguoi dung nao thay gi` trong dashboard.
- Loai bo cac muc linh tinh, trung lap, khong phuc vu cong viec hom nay.
- Lay day lam nguon chuan de don sidebar, workspace va cac dashboard con.

## Nhom nguoi dung chot
1. Quan tri he thong
2. Lanh dao
3. Quan ly
4. Nhan vien kinh doanh
5. Nhan vien marketing
6. Nhan vien tai chinh
7. Nhan vien nhan su
8. Nhan vien phap ly
9. Nhan vien website / CMS
10. Cong tac vien / Dai ly

## Nguyen tac chot
- Moi vai tro chi thay nhung thu phuc vu cong viec cua vai tro do.
- Moi dashboard chi co 3 lop:
  - `Tab cha`
  - `Tab con`
  - `Loi vao chinh`
- Khong nhom nao duoc mang qua nhieu tinh nang quan tri he thong neu khong can.
- Khong de mot vai tro thay dashboard cua vai tro khac chi de “day du”.

## Cac muc de xuat bo khoi dashboard
- Cac muc “cho du” nhung khong phuc vu cong viec hom nay.
- Cac tab chi co tinh chat he thong ky thuat o role khong can.
- Cac tab trung nghia voi nhau nhu:
  - Tong quan trung lap
  - Bao cao trung lap
  - KPI trung lap
  - Form / tracking / CMS bi tach qua manh
- Cac loi vao generic dan den man chung thay vi man theo role.

## Nguyen tac don dashboard sau khi chot
1. Moi role chi giu 4-8 tab cha toi da.
2. Moi tab cha chi giu 3-6 tab con toi da.
3. Moi man tong chi co 1 viec uu tien so 1.
4. Moi card / KPI phai bam duoc va ra dung noi.
5. Neu mot muc khong giup nguoi dung “lam viec hom nay” thi dua xuong sau hoac bo.
